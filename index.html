Most people are interested in buying a house, and some people have concerns about house value changes.

This scene shows the trend of the average prices of properties in the United States from 1961 to 2021.
Since 1963, Property price has increased parallel to the increase of the real estate market.
Except some special cases, such as the Subprime Mortgage Crisis in 2007-2008, when property prices plummeted.
Overall, the average price of price went up by 2200 percent over 58 years.
